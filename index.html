Our Story

Born on the Fairway,
Built for the Heat

Blowin' Birdies started with a scorching summer round in Phoenix — and one golfer's epiphany that no one should have to choose between staying cool and playing the game they love.

  • 🌬️ We spent two years testing 40+ fan configurations on real golf carts across Arizona, Florida, and Georgia courses before launching our first product.
  • 🏌️ Every Blowin' Birdies system is co-designed with club pros, cart mechanics, and everyday golfers to ensure it fits real needs — not lab specs.
  • 🌱 We use recycled ABS housings and ship carbon-neutral. Staying cool shouldn't cost the planet.
